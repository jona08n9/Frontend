About the project

In this project, we worked with colors, contrast and light/dark mode for users

My solution

For my solution i wanted to use just simple light & dark colors, with three accent colours, but i realized i needed some more for the darkmode function to work

The issue arised when i wantend to switch to dark mode, and i had no option to make the the whit bakcground be darker withour just getting a complete dark site.
By adding two extra colors (a darker light colour and a lighter dark colour) I could implement the darkmode

The accent colors worked well witht the darker colors, but not the lighter. So even though the contrast wasn't great, I just made sure i used the best one on the lighter areas.
